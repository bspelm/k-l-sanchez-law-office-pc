Common New York Construction Accident Injuries

Construction workers are a vital part of the workforce in New York, but they can be among the most vulnerable to work-related injuries and fatalities. The complexities of the work and the use of heavy equipment can contribute to the seriousness and, at times, catastrophic nature of the injuries suffered by those who do this type of work. If you or someone you know is injured in a construction accident, consulting with a construction accident attorney Queens can be crucial to understanding what legal options may be available.

According to the latest data from the NYC Department of Buildings (DOB), there were 96 injuries and five deaths on construction sites across all five NYC boroughs in February alone. This puts the year on track to exceed the number of work-related accidents and deaths in 2018, which was the deadliest year for construction workers since the recession began. A construction accident attorney Queens can provide legal guidance and representation to those affected by such accidents.

Many types of injuries occur on construction sites, ranging from lacerations and broken bones to paralysis and even death. Some of the most serious and common include falls, electrocution, being struck by moving vehicles or equipment, and injuries caused by exposure to hazardous materials. In cases of severe injuries, a construction accident attorney Queens can help victims seek compensation for medical bills, lost wages, and other damages.

One of the most tragic and deadly causes of construction-related injuries is a fall from a height. This can be caused by scaffolding that is unstable or unguarded, improperly trained workers, or simply poor equipment. Falls from a height can lead to life-altering injuries such as paralysis or even death, and they can be particularly devastating for construction workers, who spend most of their time on high-rise buildings in New York City. Engaging a construction accident attorney Queens can be essential in pursuing claims for such accidents.

Another common type of injury is a slip or trip. In addition to falling off of ladders or scaffolding, these types of accidents can be caused by slippery floors or surfaces. They can also result from improperly anchored or secured ladders, stairs, ramps, and sidewalk sheds. When injuries result from slips and trips, a construction accident attorney Queens can assist in holding the responsible parties accountable.

The constant movement on a construction site means that tools and equipment are always being moved, lifted, or transported. It’s crucial that these items are properly stored and anchored, as well as that workers are aware of where they are on the site at all times. A small mishap – like a dropped wrench or an unsecured brick – can turn into a fatal accident when the worker is not paying attention. For this reason, it’s critical that these workers are provided with the proper safety gear and are adequately trained to operate heavy machinery. This includes being familiar with the operating instructions of their equipment, as well as knowing the proper way to erect or dismantle a piece of equipment. This ensures that the workers are always prepared to respond in a quick and effective way in case of an emergency. A construction accident attorney Queens can help ensure that all safety regulations are followed and that workers' rights are protected. 

Legal Steps After New York Construction Accidents

A construction site is a dangerous place where workers face many risks. While most employers take steps to protect their employees, carelessness and negligence can lead to a tragic accident that leaves someone seriously injured. If you’ve been hurt in a construction accident in Queens, consulting with a skilled construction accident attorney Queens can significantly enhance your chances of securing fair compensation for your injuries.

The first step after a workplace injury is to seek medical attention. A doctor will be able to diagnose your injuries and provide you with the proper treatment. You should also contact your employer and report the incident. The sooner you file a claim, the easier it will be to secure medical benefits and other forms of assistance. A construction accident attorney Queens can guide you through this process to ensure that all necessary steps are properly followed.

You should also speak with witnesses who may have seen the accident. Ask for their names and contact information. They might be able to provide a statement for your case later on. It’s also important to take pictures of the scene and your injuries as soon as possible, in order to preserve evidence. A construction accident attorney Queens can use this evidence to build a strong case on your behalf.

If you are unable to do this yourself, ask a friend or family member to do it for you. It is crucial to document your injuries because this will be a key piece of evidence when you file a claim or lawsuit. Documenting your injuries will allow you to prove how severe your injuries are and what effects they’ve had on your life. A construction accident attorney Queens can help ensure that all documentation is thorough and legally robust.

In addition to seeking medical treatment, you should also contact a New York City construction accident attorney as soon as possible. Your lawyer will help you file a workers’ compensation claim or personal injury lawsuit to recover damages for your injuries. He or she will review medical records and other documents, tallying your expenses and losses, identifying potential responsible parties, and assessing the overall value of your case. A construction accident attorney Queens is particularly adept at navigating the legal complexities specific to New York City and Queens.

Depending on the circumstances of your accident, you might be entitled to additional compensation in addition to workers’ compensation. You can pursue a third-party liability lawsuit to recover compensation for any additional expenses or losses. This may include funeral costs, loss of companionship and future earnings if you’re permanently disabled as a result of your injuries. Engaging a construction accident attorney Queens ensures that all potential avenues for compensation are explored.

It’s also important to note that the statute of limitations will play a role in your claim. Most personal injury claims have a limit of two or three years. Failing to file a claim within the legal time frame will mean you’ll forfeit your right to receive compensation. A construction accident attorney Queens will keep track of these critical deadlines and ensure that your claim is filed timely.
